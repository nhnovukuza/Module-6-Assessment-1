GIRAFFE

The giraffe is the world's tallest land animal. They have long neck and long legs both of which provide the height needed to reach tress from which they obtain browse. Their iconic pattern of spots cober the body with the coloration being variable across their range based on diet and other factors. The color may vary between yellow to almost black for the patches with lines whichare a yellowish-white in between. These coloration diefferences are used to divide the giraffe in to multiple sybspecies.
